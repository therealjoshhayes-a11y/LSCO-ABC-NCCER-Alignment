Building Construction Technology, AAS

Return to: Programs by Pathway Type

The Building Construction Technology Associate of Applied Science will expose students to skills needed in the field of construction management and prepare students to manage and/or own a construction business or to transfer and pursue a Bachelor of Applied Arts and Science degree in Building Construction Technology or Construction Management.

 

First Semester

  • CNBT 1210 - Basic Construction Safety Credits: 2

  • Choose One: 

  • OSHT 1405 - OSHA Regulations - Construction Industry Credits: 4

  • OR

  • CNBT 1413 - Concrete I Credits: 4

  • OR

  • CNBT 2410 - Commercial/Industrial Blueprint Reading Credits: 4
  • CNBT 2315 - Construction Specifications and Contracts Credits: 3
  • ARCE 1303 - Architectural Materials and Methods of Construction Credits: 3
  • CNBT 1316 - Construction Technology I Credits: 3
  • CNBT 1318 - Construction Tools and Techniques Credits: 3

Semester Hours: 18

Second Semester

  • CNBT 1350 - Construction Technology II Credits: 3
  • WDWK 1313 - Cabinet Making I Credits: 3
  • WDWK 2351 - Cabinet Making II Credits: 3

  • Choose One:

  • ELPT 1329 - Residential Wiring Credits: 3

  •      OR

  • CNBT 1353 - Construction Technology III Credits: 3

Semester Hours: 12

Third Semester

  • CNBT 1266 - Practicum - Construction Engineering Technology Credits: 2
  • CNBT 2344 - Construction Management II Credits: 3
  • CNBT 1300 - Residential & Light Commercial Construction Drawings Credits: 3
  • CNBT 1346 - Construction Estimating I Credits: 3
  • CNBT 1411 - Construction Materials and Methods I Credits: 4

Semester Hours: 15

Fourth Semester

  • EDUC 1300 - Learning Framework Credits: 3
  • ENGL 1301 - Composition I Credits: 3
  • MATH 1332 - Contemporary Mathematics (Quantitative Reasoning) Credits: 3

  • OR MATH 1314  OR MATH 1342  

  • Choose One:

  • Lang Phil Cult Core - Language, Philosophy, and Culture Core Credits: 3

  •      OR

  • Cr Arts Core - Creative Arts Core Credits: 3
  • Soc Beh Sci Core - Social and Behavioral Science Credits: 3

Semester Hours: 15

Total Program Hours: 60
